How to Place an Order

We keep the ordering process straightforward. No accounts to create, no complicated checkout. Here's how most customers order from us.

1
Browse our products
Check out our product catalog to see what we carry across foil, fiber, paper, plastic and PPE categories. Most items are available by the case.
💡 Don't see something? Call us — we carry more than what's listed and can often source specific items.
2
Call or email us with your list
The fastest way to order is by phone: (347) 636-6182. Tell us what you need and how much, and we'll confirm availability and pricing right away.

Prefer email? Send your order list to jingchengtradinginc@gmail.com and we'll follow up promptly.
📱 You can also send us a message on WhatsApp — photos of products welcome if you're not sure of the name.
3
We confirm your order & delivery
We'll confirm your items, total, and a delivery window. We primarily serve Queens and Long Island, with reach across all five boroughs. Call to confirm delivery to your address.
4
Delivery to your door
We deliver directly to your business. Most orders within our primary area are fulfilled within the same week. Payment is collected at delivery or by prior arrangement.
🕐 Order earlier in the week for fastest turnaround.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum order?
We don't have a strict minimum, but most items are sold by the case. Give us a call and we can work with you on quantities.
How long does delivery take?
Most orders within Queens and Long Island are fulfilled within the same week. Orders placed earlier in the week typically ship faster. Call us for a more specific timeframe.
Do you deliver to all five boroughs?
Our primary delivery area is Queens and Long Island, but we do reach all five boroughs. Call us at (347) 636-6182 to confirm delivery to your specific address.
How do I pay?
Payment is collected at delivery or by prior arrangement. Contact us to discuss payment options for your account.
Can I order a product not listed on your website?
Yes — we carry more than what's listed on the site and can often source specific items. Give us a call or send us a photo on WhatsApp and we'll let you know.
Do you offer wholesale pricing?
Yes, all of our pricing is wholesale. We work directly with restaurants, supermarkets, delis and cafes on volume orders.
